Who are we?

SpaceJump

SpaceJump is a subsidiary of SpaceX created and funded by Elon Musk. We offer both classic low altitude skydives and skydives from space, i.e. 100,000km from the earth for a drop of more than 20 minutes. Very few people have yet had the chance to jump from space, but thanks to our affiliation with SpaceX, we have reusable and guided rockets to send you into the Mesosphere (50 km from Earth).
